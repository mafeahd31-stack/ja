# جايـكم - Logo Design Specification

## Colors
- Primary Green: #00843D (Syrian flag green)
- Gold Accent: #D4AF37
- Black: #000000
- White background

## Elements
- Rounded square app icon shape
- Green location pin centered above text with 3 motion speed lines
- Gold curved arc above the logo
- Bold modern Arabic "جايكم" in center
- Thin Arabic "طلبات توصيل" subtitle below
- Curved underline: green left, black right

## Style
- Minimalist flat UI design
- Vector-style
- No gradients, no 3D, no stars
- Clean white background
- Soft shadow
